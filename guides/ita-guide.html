ITA Test Guide

International Teaching Assistant Oral English Test

Disclaimer (as of 202508): This is not an official note, only a kind reminder.

Before Taking the ITA Test:

  • Ensure you have been assigned as a Teaching Assistant
  • Check your eligibility requirements
  • Review the test format and expectations
  • Practice your oral English skills

Test Information:

  • Test duration: Approximately 45 minutes
  • Format: Oral interview with English Language Center staff
  • Location: English Language Center
  • Required for: International TAs who will interact with students and language scores (e.g., TOFOL) less than a certain bar

Additional Important Information

  • If you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, this can be done through your Bookings appointment confirmation email or by contacting our office at txglobal-ita@austin.utexas.edu. Appointments must be rescheduled or canceled at least 72 hours in advance to avoid a no-show fee (billed through What I Owe).
  • ITA Screening Directions: Review this document for task instructions and information about scores and results.
  • ITA Screening Exemptions: Students with a score of 25 or higher on the speaking section of the TOEFL iBT or a 7.5 or higher on the speaking section of the IELTS within the last 24 months can be exempted. Contact us before your appointment if you have that score.
  • Screening Day Issues: If you have a true emergency or technical issue on your screening day, contact the ITA Screening & Certification Team (+1 512-471-2480 or txglobal-ita@austin.utexas.edu).
